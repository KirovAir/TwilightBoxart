This browser cannot save onto your SD card

Chrome or Edge on a computer can write straight onto the card; this browser cannot, and that will not change. You can still use this page: pick your card below, and at the end you get one .zip to copy onto the card yourself. Or use the desktop app, which works everywhere.

The full story is in the browser support notes.
